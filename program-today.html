Brain Upgrade

Meaning

  • All negative emotions come from two sources:
    • Unfulfilled needs
    • A map saying you need to feel that way
  • We can remove negative maps by showing our brain how they actuallyare not helping us meet our 6 Human Needs
  • We can create new maps and habits by showing our brain why the newmap/habit will help us meet our 6 Human Needs
  • Often this process results in immediate change, and sometimes thereare deeper maps or more engrained neural pathways to explore.

Actions

  • Identify Map Causing Negative
  • Deframe Inhibiting Map.
  • Reframe Beneficial Map in its Place
