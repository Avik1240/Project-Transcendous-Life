Our Story

We believe that individuals have infinite potential.

In 2018, Karen and Bryson both studied NAAP (Neuro Auto-Associative Programming), and it opened their minds to the possibilities for neuro empowerment and changed the way they saw the world. As they started incorporating these principles, along with other principles of neuroplasticity, they began to see incredible changes take place in their own lives. They recognized that the majority of challenges that individuals faced were created by or even maintained by their own minds, and that by helping individuals remove mental blocks and replace them with powerexpanding neuro pathways and habits, they could help thousands expand their personal power and change their own lives.

But trying to incorporate all these principles all at once is challenging and overwhelming, so they began discussing and researching how to use these very principles to make the process natural and effective.. They ultimately decided that the best way to share this powerful knowledge was through a guided course paired with a mobile application. This would be the only way to both present complex neuroscience principles while also helping individuals gradually shift their mental state and develop life-altering habits as they learn to create and move towards their personal vision, unhindered by their own brains. Karen and Bryson began developing the Live Free program in 2020, combining years of neuroscience education, clinical neurology experience, and applied research.

The challenge comes when we let outside forces shape our mental pathways because we don't understand how our brain works and are then left struggling with the results.

Our Vision

A community of empowered individuals with full access to their infinite potential to live free.

  • A community of individuals free of any internal conflict or mental roadblocks.

  • Unity of conscious desires and subconscious pathways allowing vision to become reality.
